About Liquid Mercenary

Liquid Mercenary was built on decades of hospitality experience, professionalism, and a passion for creating memorable events. Our lead bartender brings over 20 years of bartending and management experience, including event work with the Left Coast chapter of the Bartenders Guild, supporting events such as Repeal Day celebrations and Tiki events. We provide a polished, dependable bar experience designed around your guests, your event, and your vision.

Every booking includes a professional team. While Liquid Mercenary is led by an experienced bartender, our support team ensures smooth service, preparation, and cleanup from start to finish.
